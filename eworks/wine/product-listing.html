Sunbrella Outdoor Fabrics

Our variety of super practical, super stylish lightweight outdoor planters includes a wide range of styles, sizes and shapes to help you curate a gallery of botanical beauty in any outdoor environment. All-weather, fiberglass planters have a multi-functional appeal. They're designed to help you keep each and every plant right where you need it thanks to their lightweight build and are treated for all weather systems for supreme durability. Like most planters, fiberglass pots should be kept off the ground during the winter months, but many of our styles already include feet so you can keep them outside all year round.

These lightweight planters are made by top brands like AuthenTEAK and Capital Garden to give you an unbeatable selection of designs to choose from. We carry modern planters with streamlined, straight-line designs in addition to traditional, deco and Greek-inspired pots that speak to your inner classicalist. We even carry intriguing beehive planters, garden urns and vase planters to help you achieve the exact architectural aesthetic that works with your outdoor environment. Many of these all-weather planters ship free and come with two-year warranties for added peace of mind.
